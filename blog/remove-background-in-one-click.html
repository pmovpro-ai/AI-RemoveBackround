Remove Background in One Click: Behind the AI Technology

Achieving a background-free photo used to require complex selections. Today, the entire process takes one click and under 2 seconds. The underlying technology consists of browser-level AI executing highly compressed models.

How the AI Separates Subjects

The AI model performs semantic segmentation. It examines each pixel in the image and calculates the probability that it belongs to the background or foreground. A boundary mask is generated, and the background pixels are faded to 0% opacity instantly.
