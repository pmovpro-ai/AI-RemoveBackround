How to Change Background Color of Image Online Instantly

Simply making a background transparent is not always the end goal. Frequently, editors need to replace the transparent pixels with a clean solid color to fit specific document requirements or match brand guidelines.

Popular Color Presets for Photos

Different use cases demand specific background tones:

  • White: The universal standard for product listings and passport photos.
  • Blue / Red: Frequently used for official employee badges and school ID pictures.
  • Custom Gradients: Used in corporate portraits to add a modern, professional flare.
